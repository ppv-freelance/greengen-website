Energy Services
We offer the following testing and commissioning services.

Substation Services

GreenGen Inc. is committed to providing quality service to ensure that transformers and other substation equipment are 100% reliable. Our substation services include:

  • Substation Maintenance and Testing
  • Substation Upgrading and Retrofit of Existing switchgear
  • Substation Pre-Commissioning, Commissioning and Start-up
  • Acceptance Test of Electrical and Mechanical component of Switchgear i.e. HV, MV and LV.
  • Gas Insulated Switchgear
  • Metal Clad Switchgear
  • Power Transformer
  • Switch Yard, GIS Circuit Breaker, Disconnector, Earth Switch, Surge Arrestor, CVT, etc.
  • SF6 Gas Testing and Handling
  • Power System Automation ( SCADA/HMI)
  • Protection Relay and Metering Test
  • Substation Trouble Shooting
Transformer Oil Testing & Analysis

Transformer oil is pivotal in the cooling procedure of a transformer. Regular Transformer Oil Testing & Analysis is recommended to ensure a safe workplace and GreenGen Inc. provides complete solution in transformer insulating oil analysis and testing such as the following:

  • Moisture Content
  • Dielectric Strength
  • Acidity
  • Interfacial surface Tension
  • Dissipation Factor and resistivity
  • Relative Density
  • Specific Gravity
  • Visual Inspection
  • Polychlorinated biphenyls (PCB) content
  • Dissolved gas analysis (DGA)
  • Insulating Oil Reconditioning and Filtration
Consultation Services

GreenGen Inc.’s consultation services include coordination studies using ETAP Software and complete design when upgrading an old substation to a modern substation using advanced Intelligent Electronic Device (IED) and Advance Metering Infrastructure (AMI).
