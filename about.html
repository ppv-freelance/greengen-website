About Us

Who we are

GreenGen Inc. was registered in Security and Exchange Commission (SEC) Philippines in the third quarter of 2015 in association with a foreign company Digital Power Technology at the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

GreenGen Inc. was established, forged and equipped with people that is technically knowledgeable, has passion and commitment to their work, professional and has the capability to provide quality service/products to meet the client’s satisfaction. With the emergence of new industrial technologies from all over the globe, GreenGen Inc. aims to provide the best solution in each and every field of profession we excel, from renewable energy to energy services. We at GreenGen Inc., with our highly technical and well experienced professionals want to provide the best solution in a “greener way”.

Mission

to deliver high quality service which conforms to the customer requirements. Thereby, providing customer satisfaction.

Vision

to be the leading Filipino company in providing innovative and practical solutions to the electric power industry and to be the premier choice of the Filipino community in taking the step towards achieving a green economy.

Our commitment to Quality Services is unreserved
GreenGen Inc. is committed to the objective that each and every client will benefit only the best quality services and/or products. We aim to achieve this by utilizing modern technology and timely and competitive proposal from our every professional without reservations.

Major considerations in order to meet our main objective are the following:

  • Customer Feedback
  • Value for Money
  • Top of the line and state-of-the-art tools and equipment
  • Experienced Engineers with Technical Expertise
  • Result-Oriented
  • Time-conscious project implementation
