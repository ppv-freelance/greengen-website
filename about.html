About Us
Get to know us
Why Choose Greengen

Our company is focused on providing well designed both grid tied and off-grid solar systems that are made of high quality materials to residential, commercial, non-profit and government institutions. Our full service includes monitoring, design, installation and integration of solar systems, which will be executed by our highly experienced engineering team. We want everyone to experience the benefits of the efficient use of clean energy such as reduced carbon footprint and lower electricity bill. Moreover, our company also aims to provide electricity especially to our fellow Filipinos living in the remote areas of The Philippines.

GreenGen Inc. is proud to be your partner in taking the first step towards a bright and sustainable energy future. Kindly contact us for free consultation and other inquiries.

Mission

to provide renewable and sustainable energy solutions to the world’s expanding energy needs at a reasonable price so that we could help as many people as possible in creating a brighter future.

Vision

to be the leading Filipino company in providing innovative and practical solutions to the electric power industry and to be the premier choice of the Filipino community in taking the step towards achieving a green economy.
